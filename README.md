# Vitale — Your Personal Health Agent

Vitale is a free, private AI-powered health coaching web app built on Claude AI. 
It interviews you about your health, builds a personalized profile based on your 
biology, goals, and life stage — then acts as your daily health coach for meal 
planning, nutrition tracking, and wellness habits.

## What it does

- Conducts a guided health interview to understand your unique needs
- Builds a science-based personal health profile (calories, macros, nutrients)
- Acts as a daily coach — meal suggestions, food logging, macro tracking
- Tracks daily habits (water, movement, sleep, supplements)
- Stores your profile in your browser — private, no account needed

## How to use it

1. Open the app at: https://absurdrobot.github.io/vitale
2. Enter your Anthropic API key (get one free at console.anthropic.com)
3. Complete your health interview — takes about 10 minutes
4. Start your daily coaching session

## Privacy

Your API key and health profile are stored only in your own browser. 
Nothing is sent to any server other than Anthropic's AI directly.

## Built with

- Claude AI (Anthropic)
- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies
